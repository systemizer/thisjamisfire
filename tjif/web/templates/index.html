How it Works

Post it, Share it, and Discover

Post it

You know that song you've been listening to on repeat for the past couple days? Why not share it with others? Post the song to This Jam Is Fire and we'll notify your followers!

Share it

Share your song on Twitter. This Jam Is Fire will help you tell the world about your new jam and we'll setup a page for your new masterpiece so others can listen.

Discover

Don't have any jams right now? Discover other people's jams on This Jam is Fire. Follow those who post the best jams so you can keep up to date with awesome music.
